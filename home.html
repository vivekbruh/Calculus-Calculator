About


Hello everyone. My name is Vivek Narayana and I created CalcFix Calculator. This online calculator is currently capable of solving things like derivatives, limits, and integrals. There is also an experimental series expansion tab. It still works, but it's just unable to put the expansion into the form of an infinite series. This calculator is capable of doing all the basics from Calc I and II. The integrals tab is placed under the Calc I section, but it's still able to do integration by parts, partial fraction integration, trigonometric integration, and integration by trigonometric substition. It also supports improper integrals, and can state whether the integral converges or not.
